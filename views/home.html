Main Features

WOOBUS believes that every traveller deserves to be wooed during the journey. Therefore, we focus on ensuring a safe, comfortable and joyous bus journey.

LEG SPACE

Each bus is customized in a way to provide sufficient leg room for every one. We keep exclusive seats for our extra tall travellers.

CACHEFI WIFI

Our own smart WiFi router to provide movies, songs, travel guides on the go to ensure a joyous journey even in the absence of internet.

ACCESSORIES

Clean blankets, Eye masks, Ear buds, Puke bags, Sanitary napkins and etc.

TRACKING

Each bus has GPS installed in it which provides real time tracking of the bus accessible both by travellers and their friends & family.
